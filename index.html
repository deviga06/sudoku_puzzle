RULES TO PLAY

 The Sudoku puzzle is a 9x9 grid, divided into 9 smaller 3x3 subgrids (or boxes).
Your goal is to fill in the grid with numbers from 1 to 9 such that:
Each row contains all the numbers from 1 to 9 (no repetition).
Each column contains all the numbers from 1 to 9 (no repetition).
Each 3x3 subgrid contains all the numbers from 1 to 9 (no repetition).
